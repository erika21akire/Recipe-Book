Grilled Shrimp Tacos with Avocado Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • Â¼ cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, toss shrimp with olive oil, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a grill pan or outdoor grill over medium-high heat.
  3. Grill shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until pink and opaque.
  4. In another bowl, combine avocado, tomato, red onion, cilantro, and lime juice to make the avocado salsa.
  5. Warm tortillas on the grill for a few seconds on each side.
  6. Assemble the tacos by adding grilled shrimp and topping with avocado salsa.
